❓ Help Center

Find answers to common questions or get in touch with our support team.

How do I create an account? +
Click on "Get Started" or "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your personal information, choose a username and password, select your interests, and agree to our terms. Your account will be ready instantly!
Is membership free? +
Yes! Basic membership is completely free. You get access to community features, networking, and select free courses. Premium courses and advanced features may require a subscription.
I forgot my password. What should I do? +
Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.
How do I enroll in a course? +
After logging in, go to the Courses section or Dashboard. Browse available courses and click "Enroll" on any course you are interested in. You can start learning immediately after enrollment.
Can I connect with other members? +
Absolutely! Use the Network section to find and connect with other members. You can filter by location, skills, and experience level. Send connection requests and start building your professional network.
How do I update my profile? +
Go to your Dashboard and click on "Profile" in the sidebar, or click on your avatar in the top right corner and select "My Profile". You can update your photo, bio, skills, and other information there.
Are the events virtual or in-person? +
We host both virtual and in-person events. Virtual events are accessible from anywhere, while in-person events are held in various cities across Northern Nigeria. Check the Events section for details on each event.
How can I become a mentor? +
If you have significant experience in tech, you can apply to become a mentor. Go to your Profile settings and select "Apply as Mentor". Our team will review your application and get back to you within 5 business days.
